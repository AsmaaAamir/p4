Macarons


Macarons are a type of cookie that has become popular worldwide. It refers to the French or Parisian egg white foam-based cookie. This cookie is the French take on the original macaroon Macarons are small, delicate and meringue-based cookies. They have a crunchy exterior and soft, light, nougat-like interior.Macarons can be recognized by their characteristic slightly domed top and a ruffled base known as the foot.
